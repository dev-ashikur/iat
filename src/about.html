About Us

Established in 2014, IAT Solutions is a proud Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB) committed to delivering innovative IT services tailored to the unique needs of federal government agencies. With over a decade of experience providing reliable solutions to a range of federal clients, we have built a strong reputation for excellence, integrity, and trust.

Over the past 10 years, we have specialized in supporting federal agencies through services such as IT Networking, IT Operations Management, Automated Network Solutions, and Cloud Integration. By offering scalable, secure, and efficient solutions, we empower agencies to modernize their infrastructure, streamline operations, and meet mission-critical objectives. Our expertise, combined with a deep understanding of the public sector’s unique demands, allows us to consistently deliver results that exceed expectations.

At IAT Solutions, we are a trusted partner in achieving success. Through the strategic use of cutting-edge technology and industry best practices, we help federal agencies enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and remain compliant with evolving regulations. Guided by our core values of reliability, innovation, and excellence, we remain steadfast in our mission to support the critical work of our nation’s government.
